Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) was developed by Dr. Aaron Beck and introduced to a popular audience in Dr. David Burns' bestselling book "Feeling Good." This app is largely based around the principles described therein. The three principles that form the foundation of CBT are:

1) All of your moods are created by your "cognitions," or thoughts.
2) When you are feeling depressed, your thoughts are dominated by a pervasive negativity.
3) These pervasive negative thoughts frequently contain gross distortions.

By identifying these cognitive distortions present in our anxious and depressive thoughts, and reframing these thoughts in a more rational and compassionate way, we can tame thoughts of self-hatred, assess challenging situations more realistically, and learn to become more confident in our powers. The other sections will describe the ten principal cognitive distortions that CBT researchers have described, as well as some concluding thoughts that will tie what you have learned together and leave you feeling ready to begin with the journal section of the app.
